Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Constarch
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/3 cup White Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Butter, Room Temperature
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 cups Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. First set the oven to 350 degrees F. Line a baking tray with parchment paper and put to the side.
  2. Mix flour with baking powder, cornstarch and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the butter and sugar.
  4. In the bowl with the butter and sugar, add eggs and vanilla extract.
  5. Slowly mix the dry ingredients in with the wet ingredients.
  6. Add in the chocolate chips.
  7. Form the dough into 1.5 in balls spread apart on the baking tray.
  8. Put in oven and let bake for 13-15 minutes.
  9. Let cool and enjoy!

Dark Chocolate Fodant Cake

Ingredients

  • 200g Dark Chocolate 70% cocoa
  • 1/2 cup Salted Butter
  • 2/3 cup Sugar
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 3 tbsp flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees farenheit and grease a 9 inch pan with butter.
  2. In a medium saucepan melt the butter and dark chocolate.
  3. Turn off the heat and stur in sugar.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, making sure to mix vigorously so the eggs don't cook.
  5. Gently mix in flour.
  6. Pour the batter into the pan
  7. Cook for 15-17 minutes
  8. Enjoy! This recipe is great warm or after it's cooled.
