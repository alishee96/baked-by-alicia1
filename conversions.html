Measurement Conversion

It can be difficult to follow a recipe if it's all in grams and you don't own a food scale.
This page is for simple conversions so you can bake any recipe you want!:

Ingredient 1 Cup 1 Tablespoon 1 Teaspoon
Flour 120g 8g 2.5g
Sugar 200g 12.5g 4g
Butter 227g 14g 4.7g
Milk 240g 15g 5g
Water 240g 15g 5g
